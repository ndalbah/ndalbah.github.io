About

I’m a Computer Science student at LaSalle College in Montréal, currently pursuing my diploma in Programming and Software Development. Through my coursework and projects, I’ve developed a strong foundation in both front-end and back-end development, working with technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, and databases. I enjoy building functional, user-friendly applications that combine clean design with efficient code.


What motivates me most is the problem-solving aspect of programming—breaking down complex challenges and turning ideas into working solutions. I’m constantly exploring new tools, frameworks, and programming practices to broaden my skill set and stay up-to-date with the evolving tech industry.


In addition to coding, I value collaboration and clear communication. Working on team projects at LaSalle has taught me how to plan, share ideas, and deliver software effectively in a group setting. My goal is to continue growing as a full-stack developer, contributing to meaningful projects that make technology more accessible and impactful for users.
